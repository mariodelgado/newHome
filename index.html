Redesigning for the small screen





When Blackboard's Mosaic platform as a service expanded to support the iPad, the app platform was redesigned and optimized for larger devices. The new design, however, was not billed as a universal solution - the more popular small-format device language was not updated alongside the support for tablets.

The small format redesign was the first visual update to the platform in over 2 years. Beyond translating the design language from a large-screen format to a small one, many new features were added and the target demographic was expanded. The previous client base of Colleges (students 18-24) has expanded to include support for K-12 institutions with an end-user demographic of parents, approximately in the age range of 35-50 years old.
The Mosaic iPad app implementation. Blackboard's clients are able to customize their own verison with custom data, news feeds, branding and image assets.
